Lazer Eyes

An Original EDM Opera

Music and Lyrics by Van Broh

Presented by Amanda + James

WHAT???

This performance presents Lazer Eyes, a dazzling, one-of-a-kind electronic dance-opera. Originally a fully produced concept album by New York-based creative collaboration Van Broh, Lazer Eyes pays heed to classical form while utilizing a variety of EDM styles. The work will be adapted to the stage in three parts by multidisciplinary production company Amanda + James.

Telling the story of a young coal miner who strikes a magic meteor, the sparks of which leave him with “lasers for eyes,” Lazer Eyes follows his journey as he becomes a hero, defending the world against the savvy media-mastermind Reflektor, who is bent on enslaving the world one digital screen at a time.
